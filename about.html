I am a researcher in palaeoecology and palynology, currently completing my PhD focused on pollen analysis and the reconstruction of past vegetation dynamics in Mediterranean environments.

My work involves multivariate statistics, compositional data transformations, and the integration of modern and fossil pollen records to investigate the relationship between vegetation change and human impact.

I also develop tools and workflows in R for data analysis and reproducible research, aiming to make palaeoecological methods more accessible to the scientific community.
