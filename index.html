Welcome!

This is my personal website hosted on GitHub Pages. Here I share research notes, analyses, and projects related to palaeoecology, palynology, and quantitative approaches to environmental reconstruction.

I am particularly interested in plant biodiversity of the past and its reconstruction, especially in wetlands, numerical ecology methods and informatic tools applied to palaeoenvironmental research.

Currently, I am enrolled at the last year of the PhD program in Models and Methods for Material and Environmental Sciences. I am carrying out my research activities at the Laboratory of Palinology and Palaeobotany of the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia, Italy. I am a member of the BRAIN - Botanical Records of Archaeobotany Italian Network, of the Thematic Working Group on Plant Biodiversity of the Past (TWG-PBP) of the Italian Botanical Society (SBI), and of the PalaeOpen COST Action.
